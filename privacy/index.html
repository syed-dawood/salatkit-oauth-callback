SalatKit Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

SalatKit (“we”, “the app”) is an iOS prayer companion developed by Syed Muhammad Dawood Shah. This policy describes how SalatKit handles information when you use the mobile application (bundle ID: com.salatkit.app).

Summary

SalatKit is designed to minimize data collection. Prayer times are computed using your location (or a location you choose manually). Qur’anic text comes from Quran Foundation’s Content API. Optional account sign-in uses Quran Foundation OAuth and stores tokens on your device only.

Information we process

  • Location — Used for prayer times, Qibla direction, and timezone-aware scheduling. You may disable GPS and enter coordinates manually in Settings.
  • Device motion — Used for the Qibla compass heading when you open the Qibla screen.
  • App preferences — Calculation method, notification settings, Arabic script choice, and similar options are stored locally on your device.
  • Quran Foundation account (optional) — If you sign in, OAuth tokens and session data are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device. We do not operate a backend server that receives your password or tokens.
  • Cached content — Prayer calendars and Qur’an content may be cached locally (SwiftData) to improve offline performance.

Third-party services

  • Aladhan API — Receives latitude/longitude (or your manual coordinates) to return prayer times and Hijri dates. See aladhan.com.
  • Quran Foundation — Provides Qur’anic text, translations, search, and optional user features (bookmarks, goals, streaks) when you sign in. See quran.foundation and their privacy practices.
  • Apple — Notifications, Live Activities, widgets, and sign-in flows use standard iOS system services.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not run advertising trackers in SalatKit.
  • We do not upload your location or Keychain tokens to a SalatKit-owned server.

Data retention & deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear its data. OAuth tokens can be removed by signing out in the Account tab or deleting the app.

Children

SalatKit is a general-audience Islamic utility app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version.
